Why choose Guildflow to run your group?

  • Unlike Meetup.com,

    If you choose not to renew your subscription, we won't sell your group to the first person who shows up with a new credit card number.

    In fact, Guildflow has a dedicated system to allow you to export and migrate your data should you find a better solution for your group as it grows and evolves. No hard feelings.

  • Unlike Facebook Events,

    Guildflow will never sell your group data to power an advertising platform.

    Why let involvement in a sensitive group, like a divorce or substance-abuse support group, influence the ads your household sees.

  • Unlike Meetup.com and Facebook Events,

    Member details, including RSVP status, are never shared on the public internet.

    Why should a current boss be able to discover someone attending a resume workshop. With Guildflow, members have real privacy controls.

  • Unlike Meetup.com and Facebook Events,

    Your group website does not live in the shell of a third-party provider.

    The focus is on your group, not the tools running it. For even more control, use a custom domain that you own.

And all of the standard meetup tools you'd expect!

  • Build and share an event calendar with RSVPs.
  • Provide a membership directory so people can learn more about each other.
  • Create a custom Membership Application to capture some background info before letting people get access.
  • Connect with other tools via a modern GraphQL API.
